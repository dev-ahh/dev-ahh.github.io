About Us

We started H2V because we see the importance of green hydrogen in the development of a world society based in the renewable energy. We bring together a group of top-tier professionals with extensive experience in the development of projects, from solar photovoltaics to large-scale export. Our goal is to produce the cheapest hydrogen in the world from solar, with an optimal solution for Atacama desert. Our mission is to create a Hydrogen Hub located in Atacama exporting renewable energy to the world, leaving Chile within the main players worldwide in H2.

Process
Overview

Paracelsus is a project of a large-scale electrolysis in the Chile’s Atacama desert, with the objective to export Green Hydrogen to the world.


The project considers a dedicated off-grid photovoltaic power plant to supply an a 2.0 GW electrolysis facility, a desalination plant to fulfill water supply, a gas pipeline to connect the green hydrogen facility to the transformation and storage plant in the Mejillones bay, and the final connection to load port.


Project Overview
And Rationale

Global energy supplied by Green Hydrogen is rising and there is an unlimited potential market for renewable energy. Our proposal is to focus on the best solar location of the world, building a sustainable chain of production and transportation of Green Hydrogen, aiming to exports to the world, developing the country and its people.

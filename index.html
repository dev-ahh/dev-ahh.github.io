Paracelsus,

a large-scale electrolysis facility

The project considers a three-phase development plan. Phase I is a 10 MW electrolysis facility, a dedicated off-grid PV Plant to supply a hydrogen powered freight train. Phase II consists of a larger 250 MW facility aimed at scaling Phase I to a wider range of local applications and Phase III consist of a 2.0 GW electrolysis facility and maritime port for export of H2 and derivatives.
